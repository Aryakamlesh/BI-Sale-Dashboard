Sales Analysis Dashboard

Introduction/Purpose --
A sales dashboard summary provides a visual, of key sales performance indicators (KPIs) like total revenue, conversion rates, to help teams track goals, identify trends, and make quick, data-driven decisions to boost sales and forecast accurately. It transforms complex data into understandable charts, graphs, and scorecards for managers, showing progress towards targets and highlighting areas needing attention towards Total revenue, growth rates, profit margins, and performance against quotas.Uses visuals to quickly spot trends, bottlenecks, and opportunities.Sales Dashboard provides High-level view of financial metrics and growth trends.
